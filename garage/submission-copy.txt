One-liner
AI interview prep mini-app on Circles — practice with voice AI, score 70+ to earn CRC, pay 2 CRC to unlock full debrief on the leaderboard.

Circles integration
- @aboutcircles/miniapp-sdk — wallet connect, account creation, CRC transfers
- requestCreateAccount() + referral invite links from in-app share kit
- constructAdvancedTransfer() — 2 CRC debrief unlock payments to IQlify org treasury
- getProfileView() — CRC balance on wallet page
- Runs in Circles playground + miniapps iframe host

Tracks
- CompetentIQ (technical)
- ReflectIQ (behavioral)
- BuilderIQ (Circles/garage builder prep)

Note for reviewers
Voice interviews require opening the app in a direct browser tab (mic blocked in iframe until Circles host adds microphone to iframe allow). Wallet, referrals, leaderboard, and CRC payments work inside the Circles host.
